Animals

Monkey

Monkey is a common name that may refer to groups or species of mammals, in part, the simians of infraorder Simiiformes.

Rhinoceros

A rhinoceros, commonly abbreviated to rhino, is one of any five extant species of the family Rhinocerotidae.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest species among the Felidae and classified in the genus Panthera. It is recognizable for its dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur.

Elephant

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantidae in the order Proboscidea. Three species are currently recognised.

Giraffe

The giraffe is a genus of African even-toed ungulate mammals, the tallest living terrestrial animals and the largest ruminants.

Lion

The lion is a species in the family Felidae; it is a muscular, deep-chested cat with a short, a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
